Aliaksandra Tucha (b. 1997, Baranovichi, Belarus) works with photographs to tell stories of closeness, home, and family from New Haven, CT, USA and Warsaw, Poland. She is an MFA candidate at the Yale School of Art (Photography ’25) and holds a BA degree from Yale College (Visual Arts: Film and Photography ’22).
